About the Artist

Jeremy Hayes

I came back to art after years away from it. Most of the work has been study work so far: reference, lighting, anatomy, mood, and technique. The goal now is to push harder into original work and build my own visual language.

The work leans toward fantasy realism. Figures. Faces. Armor. Shadow. Things that feel half remembered and half fought for.
